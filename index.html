About me

I am mostly self learning AI, machine learning, deep learning and related areas by trying to understand ideas from first principles and applying them on research and real projects

A major part of my current work is with the Microbotics Research Project, where I am working with a global team of researchers on the idea of preventing heart attack before they happen. My work here is focused on computational navigation, reinforcement learning, digital twins, swarm modelling and behaviour, and navigation policy

Alongside this, I am also involved in long term research on clinical machine learning and deep learning with Soham Biren K. under Medical AI Research Institute, MAIRI and this work is helping me explore how machine learning can be used in healthcare related problems

I am also working with the CDSS Engineering Team under Ritika Saha and Alireza Hayati as part of the AI/ML Analysis Working Group at the NASA Open Science Data Repository. The work focuses on AI/ML driven clinical decision support systems, health monitoring frameworks, agent-based architectures, and data-driven approaches for autonomous medical decision-making in deep-space environments

Apart from research, I am also exploring Open Source Development. I see open source as a good way to learn by building, understand real world codebases, and contribute to projects that are used by other people
